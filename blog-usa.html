3 tips for a great vacation

Sweeten your stay on Lummerland with a massage on your terrace or directly on the beach. Wherever you like.

Get to know the rivers and the surrounding area. Book a river tour through the mangroves, explore an island in the interior and enjoy a typical Bahian lunch.

Take a trip to the Barra. Where the river flows into the sea. Spend a relaxing time there and enjoy the solitude.
